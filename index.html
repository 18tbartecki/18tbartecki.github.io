Datify App

Last spring I was working on Datify, a web application that connects with your Spotify account, when the shutdowns began. Despite getting scattered around the world, my team worked through the unanticipated setback and delivered a product I truly love.

Datify allows you to securely create an account with Firebase Authentification, after which you can see your Spotify Top 5 Artists and Top 5 Songs from the past month. It also uses multi-threading to continuously update and show the song you're currently listening to. Check out a video demo from my team here.
